# Systems

### **Knave** (2018)
![book_knave.png\|left hsmall](/img/user/Images/Books/book_knave.png)
**Author:**  Ben Milton
**Pages**: 7
**Description**: A **system** compatible with old school fantasy modules and material, defined by its classless system that keeps tomb-raiding and adventuring simple and straightforward. 
<br>
<br>
>**Review**:
It was hard to move on from this first version of Ben Milton's classless OSR to the second edition as he nailed it the first time. Using your inventory to define your playstyle works great: the tank's bulky armor leaves no space to carry item, and the wannabe wizard with many spells is left exposed to attacks. Plus, the 100-Spell table is filled with fun spells designed for clever problem-solving.
>★★★★

### **Apothecaria** (2021)
![book_apothecaria.png\|left hsmall](/img/user/Images/Books/book_apothecaria.png)
**Author:** Anna Blackwell
**Pages**: 60
**Description**: A solo **journaling game** where you craft potions in order to support a small, tight-knit community. Using a deck of cards, you'll gather ingredients, cure your neighbors' ailments, and uncover the hidden secrets of the surrounding wilderness.
<br>
>**Review**:
>It has just the right amount of slice-of-life charm for a simple journaling game, but the upgrades and potion equipment management fail to make an interesting system, and lacks the depth to elevate it into something beyond just a decent journaling game.
>★★★

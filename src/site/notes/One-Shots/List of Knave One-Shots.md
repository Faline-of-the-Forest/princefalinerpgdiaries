When broad-shouldered mariner Barbas gets word that his old war buddy Ismark needs help, he rounds up the usual gang: Lawrence, the cowardly cook with a pet rat, Azazel, the sardonic alchemist, and Leigh, a performer whose fortunes have seen better days.

The Burgomaster is trying to sell old Ravenloft Castle, but every tradesman he hired to fix the place has been scared off by who they claim to be the legendary Count Strahd. Ismark thinks it's all ghost stories, but that's before the gang gets ambushed by _actual_ ghouls on the Old Svalich Road. Could Strahd really back from the dead, or is this some sort of spooky scheme?

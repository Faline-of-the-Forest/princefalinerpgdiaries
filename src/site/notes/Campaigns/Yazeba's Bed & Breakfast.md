#Yazebas-Bed-and-Breakfast

<div style="clear:both"></div>

```co
ROOM FOR EVERYONE.
```

```co
It is always September 15th at YAZEBA'S BED & BREAKFAST. Outside, the rain comes down splashy-crashy through the trees. Inside, someone is making tea, while someone else is arguing about whose turn it is to do the laundry, and a witch without a heart is watching it all from her favorite chair.
```

```co
You are a GUEST. You found this place the way everyone does — when you needed it most. Now you're here, in a strange house full of even strangers residents, just like you.
```

```co
You might pick berries with a boy who talks to animals. You might help a tired old sailor name the constellations. You might brave the scary basement that nobody else has. You might even sit on the porch with a friend and say nothing at all.
```

```co
The people here are odd and kind and broken in familiar ways, and if you stay long enough, they'll become the closest thing to FAMILY you've ever had.
```

```co
THERE IS ALWAYS ROOM FOR ONE MORE. (except for Gertrude)
```

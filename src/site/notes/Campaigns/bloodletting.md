# Casting

![por_unk.png\|left hsmall](/img/user/Images/Portraits/por_unk.png)
**Reginald "Reggie" Corrigan**, Ventrue / Invictus
*(portrayed by* #Richie-Tozier *)*
Reggie runs Blood Haven, a nonprofit blood bank in Mid-City: part genuine civic charity, part feeding operation for the Kindred of New Orleans. He sends the clean blood to medical centers, and keeps the HIV-positive units — blood that would otherwise be incinerated — for himself and vampires in need. 

He believes he is curing New Orleans of two diseases simultaneously: the poverty that leaves one in three residents without adequate food, and the hunger of the Kindred who would otherwise prey on those same people without giving anything back.


<div style="clear:both"></div>

![por_unk.png\|left hsmall](/img/user/Images/Portraits/por_unk.png)
**Andrew "Andy" Deveraux**, Malkavian / Ordo Dracul
*(portrayed by* #Geralt-of-Rivia *)*
Andy is a Vietnam veteran, a former enforcer, and a freelance Kindred bounty hunter with thirteen years of Baton Rouge between him and the city that took everything from him. He was drafted at twenty-three, served in the Mekong Delta, and came home a different man.

He was Embraced in 1975 by Clarence Beaumont, the Invictus Priscus, and spent two years as the most effective instrument in Beaumont's network until Andy killed him, giving him a reputation he used to become a prolific bounty hunter. Tonight, he is back home on personal business.

<div style="clear:both"></div>

![por_unk.png\|left hsmall](/img/user/Images/Portraits/por_unk.png)
**John "Elvys" Dresley**, Nosferatu / Unaligned
*(portrayed by* #Reepicheep *)*
John was a working guitarist from Memphis. He played rock and roll nobody wanted to hear anymore when a record producer named Shadowman offered him a studio album. Unfortunately, it was a lie, a cruel joke that led to his unwilling Embrace.

John's fiancée Virginia was four weeks pregnant when he "went missing", and after her miscarriage, Joey Tran found her in her grief and Embraced her in the spring of 1989. She came back in the autumn calling herself Eleanor, fronting a sludge metal band called Conqueror Worm, and now does not appear to remember John. For now, the Nosferatu sulks in the sewers beneath Mid-City, stalking his lost love from afar.

<div style="clear:both"></div>


![por_unk.png\|left hsmall](/img/user/Images/Portraits/por_unk.png)
**Amélie Telfort**, Daeva / Circle of the Crone
*(portrayed by* #Puss-in-Boots *)*
Amélie has run her store on the same block in the Tremé since she took over from her mother in 1920. The Telfort Herbarium sells medicinal teas and gris-gris bags by day, but, after dark, preparations incorporating her vitae are available to her Kindred peers. 

She is currently housing her latest stray, a nineteen-year-old werewolf named Caroline in the apartment above the shop, keeping her safe knowing what the Kindred of New Orleans would do if they knew a young, uncontrolled Lupine roamed the streets.

# Welcome
I have already realized that there are already not enough days in a lifetime to play all the games I'd like to try, so I do my best and run as many campaigns as my schedule allows, across as many systems and genres.

Tabletop RPGs are ephemeral by nature: they happen once, at a specific table, between specific people, and never be experienced in the same way again. Each session a lightning caught in a bottle. So, all we can do is tell their stories as if they were myths.

This archive is my attempt to give those stories a more permanent home. It chronicles the campaigns I have run, the sessions that made them, and the books and manuals I have read in pursuit of this hobby. It exists as a souvenir for everyone who joined me to committed their evenings to these games, and as a record, for myself, of the work that went into running them. 

- **Campaigns** — a breakdown of every campaign, past and ongoing
- **Sessions** — accounts of what happened at the table
- **Library** — the systems, supplements, and resources I have worked through
